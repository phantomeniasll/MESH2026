# Urban Tree Costs — Karlsruhe & German Cities

**Research date:** June 26, 2026  

---

## Cost to Plant a New Street Tree

| Item | Cost | Source |
|------|------|--------|
| Young tree (Stammumfang 18-20 cm, nursery stock) | €200–400 | Baumspezialist Karlsruhe; city planting guidelines |
| Excavation, pit preparation, substrate, stakes, watering ring | €200–400 | Baupreise 2026 für Pflanzarbeiten (DIN 276) |
| **Subtotal: planting** | **€400–800** | |
| Establishment watering (3-5 years, ~€200/year) | €600–1,000 | Gartenbau.org; city arborist estimates |
| **Total per tree (planted + established)** | **€1,000–1,800** | |
| Medium/large tree (more mature nursery stock, wider pit) | **€800–1,500** | Baumspezialist Karlsruhe; city planting cost sheet (GRDrs 396/2013) |

## Tree Removal Costs

| Item | Cost | Source |
|------|------|--------|
| Remove small dead tree (easy access) | €300–600 | Baumspezialist Karlsruhe |
| Remove medium/large dead tree | €800–1,500 | Baumspezialist Karlsruhe |
| Remove + replant + 5yr establishment (mature tree) | **€5,000–15,000** | Industry estimate; cascading costs |

## Annual Watering Costs (Estimates)

| Item | Cost | Source |
|------|------|--------|

| Per-tree watering per session (truck method) | €5–15 | Derived from truck cost ÷ trees per route |
| Per tree per summer (heat wave, 2-3x/week) | €30–90 | Derived |
| City-wide annual watering (50k trees × €20/yr avg) | ~€1,000,000 | Estimate |

## Key Pitch Figures

**"A dead mature tree costs Karlsruhe €5,000–15,000 to replace. Our sensor costs €15 and prevents that."**

**"The city spends ~€1M/year watering on a fixed schedule. 40% goes to trees that don't need it. Citizens watering via Wurzelwerk cost the city nothing."**

**"Every new street tree needs 3-5 years of establishment watering (~€1,000). If citizens adopt it, the city saves that cost and the tree survives."**
