# Commercial Soil Moisture Monitoring for Urban Trees

**Research date:** June 26, 2026

---

## Existing Products (Reference / Competition)

### Sensoterra
- **What:** Wireless soil moisture sensors for tree pits and planters
- **Key features:** Open API, 100% maintenance-free, measures moisture at multiple depths, calibrated for diverse soil types
- **Dashboard:** Data visualization, irrigation scheduling, alerting
- **Use case:** "Optimized irrigation rounds for watering trucks. Reduced tree mortality. Insurance rate reduction."
- **Images:** `references/sensoterra-tree-pit.jpg`, `references/sensoterra-smart-city.png`

### Citygreen Treebit
- **What:** IoT soil moisture monitoring for urban tree preservation
- **Key features:** Real-time alerts during dry spells, portable device moved between locations for broad soil analysis
- **Focus:** "2030 and 2050 canopy targets cannot be achieved without greater focus on existing asset preservation."
- **Website:** citygreen.com/solutions/treebit-soil-moisture-monitoring/

### TreeSense
- **What:** IoT sensors for soil moisture, crown moisture, and tree growth
- **Focus:** Municipalities, landscape architects, tree care professionals
- **Website:** treesense.de (German company)

### Smart City Dortmund
- **What:** Case study — digitalization of tree care with IoT sensors
- **Relevance:** Real German city deploying exactly what we're building
